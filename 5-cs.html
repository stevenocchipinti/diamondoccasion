The 5 "C"'s to consider when buying wholesale loose diamonds in Melbourne or anywhere else

Cut, clarity, colour, carat weight, and conflict free are the five "C's" to consider when you are purchasing stones for custom designed diamond rings or boutique jewellery from a Melbourne jeweller. As is commonly known, diamonds are forever, and it is these five qualities which can tell you if your diamonds are truly valuable. It is also a well-known fact that few can resist the sight of a diamond, but when you have a trained eye for what you should be looking for, you will know which stone constitutes a truly wise purchase.

Choosing the perfect wholesale loose diamonds becomes easy if you know what to look for. The diamond cut is what allows the stone to blaze with brilliance, while the diamond clarity, with few inclusions, allows light to pass through uninterrupted. When it comes to assessing a diamond’s carat weight, this is what determines the density of the diamond, and this is particularly crucial if you are trying to accurately work out the value of the stone. Conflict free diamonds undergo the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme, which aims to ensure that there are no stones on the market that are financing rebel movements or are harvested via a violent system.
