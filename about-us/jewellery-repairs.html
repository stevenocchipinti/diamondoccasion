Jewellery Repairs

Jewellery Repairs in Melbourne

Even top quality jewellery can break – whether it be the clasp of a bracelet or the chain on a necklace. Here at Diamond Occasion, we understand that special pieces cannot simply be replaced. Many rings, bracelets and necklaces are 'one-offs', or pieces that have long been out of production, while others are simply too expensive to re-purchase. When irreplaceable jewellery breaks, you need to be able to get it fixed, and to cater for this need we offer a quality jewellery repair service in our Melbourne CBD studio. With over 30 years of experience in the jewellery trade, we can fix just about anything. Our repair jobs are virtually undetectable, as we use high quality materials to ensure your precious piece looks just as beautiful as ever before.

When you enlist our help you are dealing directly with the craftsman, meaning you can discuss the repair job before it's carried out. Our direct service also means your jewellery is fixed onsite, rather than sent off to another location – speeding up the repair time and ensuring your piece is kept safe. By doing the work ourselves we are able to offer a very affordable service, ensuring the repair cost does not outweigh the value of the jewellery in question.

If your favourite bracelet is lying broken on your bedside table rather than adorning your wrist, contact Diamond Occasion today. We offer jewellery repairs in the Melbourne CBD, so drop by for a consultation with our master jeweller today.
