Diamond Occasion is a quality wholesale jeweller in Melbourne’s CBD

At Diamond Occasion, we specialise in wedding rings, engagement rings, fine diamond jewellery, prestige complementary jewellery and also certified loose diamonds and pearls. We understand that many people want a piece of jewellery that is unique, for example they may want their wedding ring made to complement the one received for their engagement. This is why we specialise in being able to custom make to specific requirements. With over 30 years of experience in jewellery design and manufacture, we work closely with you, one on one, to select or create that unique piece of jewellery that is "just perfect".

We sell stunning rings suitable for the most discerning buyers

At Diamond Occasion, we are proud to offer a fabulous range of wedding rings Melbourne brides will adore. Our range includes traditional bands through to more unusual and unique designs. We offer wedding rings in nine or eighteen carat yellow, white or rose gold and platinum. We also specialise in diamond rings that ladies would absolutely love to receive for their engagement. Whether you choose something we have in stock or decide to get a ring designed, our aim is to ensure that you find the perfect engagement ring. We also have fantastic semi-precious stones available if you are looking for something a bit different. If you are looking for something a little extra to complement that stunning ring, we also make diamond earrings. Melbourne brides have never been more spoilt for choice!

As timeless classics, Princess Cut engagement rings are perpetual favourites. We stock a great range for our clients who are looking for stones that are stunning as solitaires but are also versatile enough to look equally brilliant when set with other diamonds or gems. Whether you are looking at clusters, trilogies or solitaires – these diamonds always sparkle.

At Diamond Occasion, we have the unique advantage of being able to provide our clients with an extraordinary range of loose diamonds that other Melbourne businesses simply cannot match.
