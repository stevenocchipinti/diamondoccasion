Certification

The Diamonds that we sell are Certified by GIA, IGI, EGL, Auscert and HRD.

Select certified diamonds to buy for your loved ones.

Certification provides legitimacy to the source of the diamond, they ensure the diamond is traded from an authorized source.
Your most valuable possession as a diamond should be legitimate and should have quality which an authorized certification can provide.

Diamond certification also provides a lab tested diamond with accurate annalysis of Cut, Colour, Clarity and Carat Weight.
